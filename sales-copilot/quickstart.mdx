This guide walks you through creating your first deck, setting up branding, and sending a homeowner their personalized presentation link.

## Before you start

Make sure your organization has been provisioned in Sales CoPilot. If you're not sure, contact your Demand IQ account manager.

## Step 1: Create a deck

Log in and you'll land on your deck dashboard. Click **New Deck** in the top right.

Fill in the details:

1. Give your deck a **name** — something you'll recognize, like "Spring Solar Pitch"
2. Add a **Deck Context** — describe the audience and purpose in a sentence or two. This shapes the AI-generated narration, so the more specific you are, the better.
3. Upload your **slide images** by dragging them into the upload area. The order you add them is the order they'll appear in the presentation.
4. Click **Create & Generate**

Sales CoPilot will analyze each image, write narration scripts, and generate spoken audio. You'll see progress as it works through your slides — this usually takes under a minute.

## Step 2: Review your slides

Once generation is complete, your deck opens in the editor. Click through the **Slides** tab to review what was created.

For each slide you can:
- Edit the **narration script** directly
- Click **Regenerate Audio from Script** after making changes
- Use **Use AI to Generate Script** to have the AI rewrite it from the image

Don't worry about getting everything perfect before moving on — you can always come back and edit.

## Step 3: Set up branding

Open the **Branding** tab. This controls what homeowners see when they open their presentation.

At a minimum, set:
- Your **Company Logo**
- Your **Primary Color** to match your brand
- The **CTA Button Text** — what the "Get Started" button says on the landing page

The **Live Preview** on the right updates as you make changes so you can see exactly how it'll look.

## Step 4: Add FAQs (recommended)

Open the **FAQs** tab and click **Add FAQ** to add questions your homeowners commonly ask. Sales CoPilot matches these during live Q&A and responds with your exact wording.

A few good ones to start with:
- How long does installation take?
- Is there a warranty?
- What's the pricing / financing options?

## Step 5: Send a presentation

Open the **Presentations** tab and click **New Presentation**. Enter the homeowner's name, address, and contact details, then click **Create**.

You'll get a shareable link. Send it to the homeowner however you like — text, email, CRM, whatever fits your workflow. When they open it, they'll see a branded landing page with their name and address already in the narration.
