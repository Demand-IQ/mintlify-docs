---
title: Branding
description: Make presentations look like they came from you — your colors, logo, and messaging throughout.
---

The **Branding** tab in the template editor controls everything the homeowner sees when they open their presentation — your logo, color scheme, the chat avatar, and the landing page copy they read before hitting "Get Started."

Changes are reflected in the **Live Preview** panel on the right side of the screen as you make them.

## Company information

- **Company Name** — shown in the presentation header. Leave this empty to display only your logo.
- **Company Logo** — click to upload your logo file. This appears in the presentation header throughout the experience.

## Chat avatar and greeting

This controls how the Q&A chat introduces itself to the homeowner.

- **AI Avatar** — upload a square image (it'll be cropped to a circle). This appears in the chat bubble during Q&A.
- **Assistant Name** — the name shown in the chat header and messages, e.g. *Brian* or *Your Solar Advisor*
- **Initial Greeting Message** — the first message the homeowner sees when the chat opens. Keep it friendly and low-pressure, e.g. *"Hello! I'm here to help answer any questions you have."*

## Colors

- **Primary** — controls buttons, active states, and key highlights throughout the viewer
- **Neutral** — secondary text, borders, and subdued UI elements

Click the color swatch to open a color picker, or type a hex code directly.

## Typography

- **Primary Font (Headings)** — used for titles and your company name
- **Secondary Font (Body)** — used for body text, chat messages, and buttons

## Chat bubble colors

Customize the background color of chat messages:

- **User Messages** — the color of the homeowner's message bubbles
- **Assistant Messages** — the color of the AI response bubbles

## Call to action

- **Button Text** — the label on the main action button, e.g. *"Reserve Your Installation"* or *"View My Report"*

The CTA button is what drives homeowners to take the next step, so make it specific and action-oriented.

## Intro page

The **Intro** tab (next to Branding) lets you customize the landing page homeowners see before the presentation starts. Toggle it on to configure:

- **Background Image** — full-bleed image behind the intro screen
- **Headline** — main heading, supports personalization tokens like `{{first_name}}`
- **Subtitle** — supporting line below the headline
- **CTA Button Label** — the "Get Started" button text

A strong headline example: `"Welcome, {{first_name}}! Your personalized report is ready."`
